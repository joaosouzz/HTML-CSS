Cyberpunk genre

Cyberpunk is a genre that blends futuristic technology with dystopian society, often set in sprawling cities under a constant glow of neon lights. It’s a world where high-tech innovations coexist with social decay, and where hackers, rebels, and outcasts navigate through the shadows of towering skyscrapers. With its focus on corporate control, artificial intelligence, and human augmentation, cyberpunk explores themes of identity, freedom, and survival in a world where humanity is increasingly intertwined with machines.
